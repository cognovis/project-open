Flexibility or Control?

The philosophy of ]project-open[ is based on a assumption of responsible users, who (normally) act in the best interest of their company. This assumption allows ]project-open[ to emphasise Flexibility, keeping the system easier to use while being suitable for a broader range of company types.

So ]project-open[ tries to provide users with maximum freedom of decisions to deal with the unorderly world of real business. As a counterweight, ]project-open[ protocols all user decisions and makes them transparent to the users supervisors.

The benefit of this philosophy is a more flexible system suitable for small and medium companies and in knowledge intensive business sectors.
