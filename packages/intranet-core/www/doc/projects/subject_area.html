Subject Areas

Subject Areas serve to determine the type of language used in a translation project. The following list of subject areas reflect a default configuration. You can change this configuration if you enjoy administration rights in your application.

  • Bio - Biology
  • Biz - Business language
  • Com - Commercial
  • Eco - Economic language (talking about the economy)
  • Gen - Generic (no specific language such as newspapers)
  • Law - Legal stuff (contracts, ...)
  • Lit - Literature
  • Loc - Software Localization
  • Med - Medical
  • Mkt - Marketing
  • Tec - Generic Tecnical Language
  • Tec-Auto - Tecnical/Automotive
  • Tec-Mech. eng - Technical/Mecanical Engineering
  • Tec-Telecos
    • - Tecnical/Telecommunications (no software)
